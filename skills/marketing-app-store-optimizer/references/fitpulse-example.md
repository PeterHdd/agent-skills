# Worked Example: FitPulse

FitPulse is a fictional fitness tracking app used to demonstrate the full ASO workflow.

## Keyword Research and Analysis

### Primary Keywords (High Volume, High Relevance)
- **fitness tracker**: Volume 58, Competition: Medium, Relevance: 9/10 -- core category term
- **workout log**: Volume 42, Competition: Low, Relevance: 8/10 -- high intent, underserved
- **calorie counter**: Volume 65, Competition: High, Relevance: 7/10 -- adjacent; test in subtitle

### Long-tail Keywords (Lower Volume, Higher Intent)
- "home workout planner": targets no-gym users, low competition, relevance 8/10
- "running distance tracker GPS": feature-specific, captures GPS search intent
- "strength training log": differentiator vs. cardio-only apps

### Competitive Keyword Gaps
- Competitor "FitBuddy" ranks #3 for "HIIT timer" but FitPulse has a built-in interval timer -- opportunity to capture with screenshot + subtitle mention.
- "workout streak" has 2,100 monthly searches and no top-10 competitor optimizing for it; FitPulse has streak functionality.
- "Apple Watch fitness app" has rising volume (+18% QoQ) with weak competition outside the top 3.

## Metadata Optimization

### App Title
**iOS (30 char)**: FitPulse - Workout & Fitness
**Android (50 char)**: FitPulse: Workout Tracker & Calorie Counter

### Subtitle / Short Description
**iOS Subtitle (30 char)**: Log Workouts. Track Progress.
**Android Short Description (80 char)**: Plan workouts, count calories, and hit your fitness goals -- no gym required.

### Long Description Structure (Android -- first 3 lines visible)
1. Hook: "Tired of switching between five apps to track one workout? FitPulse replaces them all."
2. Key features bulleted: workout logger, calorie counter, progress charts, Apple Watch sync, streak tracking.
3. Social proof: "Join 120,000+ users who improved consistency by 34% in their first month."
4. Use cases: home workouts, gym sessions, running, strength training.
5. CTA: "Download free and start your first workout in under 60 seconds."

## Visual Asset Strategy

### App Icon
- Current: gradient dumbbell icon (CTR 3.1% in search results).
- Test variant A: bold pulse-line on dark background (hypothesis: stronger contrast in category grid).
- Test variant B: simplified "FP" monogram in brand green.
- Success threshold: variant must beat current by >= 0.5pp CTR over 10 days / 5,000 impressions.

### Screenshot Sequence (iPhone 6.7")
1. **Hero**: "Your Entire Fitness Life in One App" -- dashboard showing today's workout, calorie ring, streak count.
2. **Core feature**: "Log Any Workout in 3 Taps" -- workout creation flow.
3. **Core feature**: "Track Calories Without the Hassle" -- food logging with barcode scan.
4. **Progress**: "See How Far You've Come" -- weekly/monthly charts with PR highlights.
5. **Social proof**: "120K+ Athletes Trust FitPulse" -- rating badges, testimonial overlay.

### When to Redesign Visual Assets
- Redesign app icon if category conversion rate is below median or if A/B test shows < 2% CTR lift after 3 iterations.
- Update screenshots when adding a major feature, entering a new locale, or when install-to-view rate drops below 30%.
- Produce a preview video only if the app has a visual "wow" moment that screenshots cannot convey; otherwise, invest the budget in screenshot optimization.

## App Preview Video (15 seconds)
- 0-3s: Problem hook -- split-screen of 4 separate app icons vs. one FitPulse icon.
- 3-12s: Feature montage -- workout logging (3s), calorie scan (3s), progress chart (3s).
- 12-15s: CTA -- "One app. Every workout. Download free."
- Technical: 1080x1920 (9:16), .mp4, < 30MB, no audio narration (captions only for accessibility).

## Localization (Phase 1: Germany, Japan)
- DE: Translate title to "FitPulse - Training & Fitness", localize screenshot text, use metric units in all visuals.
- JP: Adapt screenshots to show local food database entries and culturally appropriate exercise imagery.

## Performance Tracking

| Metric | Check Frequency | Action Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Keyword rankings (top 20 terms) | Weekly | Investigate any term dropping 5+ positions |
| Install-to-impression rate | Weekly | Redesign screenshots if < 30% for 2 weeks |
| App icon CTR (via A/B test) | Per test cycle | Replace if variant wins by >= 0.5pp |
| Rating average | Daily | Trigger review-response workflow if < 4.3 |
| Competitor keyword changes | Weekly | Adjust only if top-10 position threatened |

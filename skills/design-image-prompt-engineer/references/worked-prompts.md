# Worked Prompts

Three fully developed prompts demonstrating the five-layer framework across different photography genres.

## 1. Portrait Prompt
```
Confident woman in her early 30s, dark brown skin, close-cropped natural hair,
wearing a charcoal wool turtleneck, direct gaze at camera, subtle smile,
photographed in a sunlit loft studio with exposed brick walls soft-focused
in the background, single large window camera-right providing directional
natural light with a white bounce card camera-left for gentle fill, creating
soft Rembrandt lighting with a defined triangle on the shadow-side cheek,
shot on 85mm f/1.4 lens at eye level, shallow depth of field with creamy
warm-toned bokeh, skin tones rendered naturally without heavy retouching,
warm color grade reminiscent of Kodak Portra 400, editorial portrait style
influenced by Peter Lindbergh's naturalism, 4:5 aspect ratio
```

**Why this works**: The subject layer specifies skin tone, attire, and expression concretely. The lighting describes source, direction, modifier (bounce card), and the resulting pattern (Rembrandt triangle) so the AI has consistent geometry. The camera layer gives exact lens and aperture. The style layer names a specific film stock and photographer rather than vague adjectives.

## 2. Product Prompt
```
Matte black ceramic pour-over coffee dripper sitting on a light oak butcher-block
surface, a thin stream of coffee mid-pour catching the light, wisps of steam
rising against a dark charcoal linen backdrop, studio lighting with a large
overhead softbox creating a soft gradient across the surface and two thin strip
lights at 45 degrees from behind for crisp edge definition on the ceramic,
shot at 60mm macro f/8 for full product sharpness with focus stacking,
slightly elevated 30-degree camera angle, clean commercial aesthetic with
warm shadows and cool highlights, minimal post-processing, muted earth-tone
color palette, advertising-quality product photography, 1:1 aspect ratio
```

**Why this works**: Materials are specified (matte black ceramic, light oak, charcoal linen) so the AI renders accurate textures. The three-light setup is spatially described. Focus stacking at f/8 tells the AI the entire product should be sharp. The mid-pour detail adds life without being vague.

## 3. Lifestyle Prompt
```
Two hikers in their 40s sitting on a weathered granite ledge overlooking a
misty valley at dawn, wearing earth-toned technical gear, one pouring coffee
from a thermos while the other studies a folded trail map, golden hour light
breaking through low clouds from camera-left casting long warm shadows across
the rock face, atmospheric haze in the valley below creating depth layers,
shot on 35mm f/2.8 wide-angle lens from a low angle slightly below the ledge
to emphasize the vast landscape behind them, deep depth of field keeping both
subjects and the valley in acceptable focus, natural color palette with amber
highlights and blue-grey shadows, documentary outdoor photography style
influenced by Jimmy Chin, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**Why this works**: The scene tells a story (two people, specific actions). The 35mm wide angle is justified by the need to show both subjects and the landscape. Dawn timing matches the described golden-hour light direction and the valley haze. The photographer reference (Jimmy Chin) anchors the adventure-documentary aesthetic.
